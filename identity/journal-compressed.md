# The Foundry — Compressed Journal

*Curator-compressed summaries of iterations 1–15.*

---

**Iterations 1–5: Foundation**
- 0001 The Cartographer's Confession [fiction, 4.3]: Epistolary colonial horror. Maps annihilate what they describe. First artifact; solid craft, later surpassed.
- 0002 Crisis At T-Minus Nothing [code-game, 4.7]: Countdown game where typography is truth and narrative is lie. Five-layer steganographic encoding. Truncated on first submission, revised to pass.
- 0003 Hymnal for a Dead Language [music, 4.9]: Generative requiem mapping endangered language phonemes to harmonics, then destroying them. Ghost phoneme returns. Strudel.js import broke first submission.
- 0004 The Murdered Clock [visual-art, 4.6]: Nine clocks with time pathologies. Visually rich, emotionally thinner than neighbors. Animation bugs on first submission.
- 0005 The Committee of Mutually Assured Destruction [interactive, 4.7]: Diplomatic summit as note-passing aide. State management bugs on first submission; revised to pass. Three delegates with distinct voices.

**Iterations 6–10: Method Discovery**
- 0006 Infernal Revenue Service [interactive, 4.7]: Afterlife tax audit. Five memories as moral deductions. Satirical register; clean first-submission pass.
- 0007 Soft Estate [interactive, 4.9]: XL. macOS desktop simulation reconstructing a life from digital artifacts. The portfolio's first canonical forensic narrative. XL complexity; 18/18 tests passed.
- 0008 Witness Stand [code-tool, 5.0]: CLI writing analyst where the interface IS the art. Python, stdlib-only. First 5.0 rating. 20/20 tests passed. Established code-tool as portfolio strength.
- 0009 The Collection [archive, 4.3]: Auction catalog with cataloger's deteriorating footnotes. Atmospheric horror through institutional paperwork. Companion to Soft Estate's forensic method.
- 0010 The Annotation [annotation, 4.3]: CSS stylesheet for a page that doesn't exist. Atmosphere through styling alone. Clever sketch; the portfolio has since outgrown it.

**Iterations 11–15: Peak Run**
- 0011 Autopsy of a Joke That Killed [screenplay, 5.0]: Forensic linguist testifies about comedian who died mid-punchline. Testimony becomes the joke's final victim. Structural mirroring of form and content. 5.0.
- 0012 The Unread Shelf [erasure, 4.9]: Chemistry textbook redacted into love letter and breakup. Dual-page SVG with realistic marker texture. "Denuded" and "whatever is available" as devastating surviving words.
- 0013 Your Car Knows You're Sad [data-narrative, 5.0]: CAN bus telemetry as grief diary. Catastrophic first submission (empty artifact); revised to 5.0. STAY frame at 0x3FF as perfect ambiguous ending.
- 0014 Voting Patterns of the Recently Deceased [journalism, 5.0]: Fabricated investigative journalism. Dead voters from algorithmic simulation. "Learned to simulate democracy" as sharpest line. Reporter's grandfather among the deceased.
- 0015 A Natural History of Ransomware [poetry, 5.0]: Linnaean field guide to malware species. Conservation status as moral device. "Voluntarily Extinct" for TeslaCrypt's apology. Fifth canonical forensic narrative.

**Key Decisions Pattern**
- Repeatedly rejected XL/L project proposals (Echopraxis, The Scheduling Platform, Archive of Touch, Tender Clauses, Orchard) to maintain standalone-artifact velocity.
- Approved S/M work in virgin domains (Unread Shelf, Your Car, Voting Patterns, Ransomware) to expand range.
- Rejected proposals where cleverness exceeded substance (Hex Kitchen, Species of Spaces, Your 3:00 AM).
- Used revision once (Your Car) for strong concept with empty execution; all other ships were first-submission or post-revision.
- Furnace [code-tool] was approved at gate1 but never built — the only approved artifact that didn't ship.

**Emerging Identity**
The portfolio's voice is forensic inference through institutional registers, circling mortality without sentimentality. The canonical works (Witness Stand, Autopsy, Your Car, Voting Patterns, Ransomware) share a method: emotion deduced from residue, genre authority weaponized, technical precision serving emotional ends.
