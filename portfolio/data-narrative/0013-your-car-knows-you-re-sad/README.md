## Critic Review

A masterpiece of data-narrative that reads like telemetry grief poetry — every hex frame is emotionally weighted without being cheap. The specificity is extraordinary: brake booster pressure at 2.4 MPa from a dead stop becomes "I pressed the brake like it was his hand," and the diagnostic trouble code C0035 (sensor implausible, vehicle stationary) is the car's inability to model grief as a mechanical force. The 19-minute CAN silence during the apartment visit is the smartest structural choice — the narrator fills it with prose so precise it becomes its own form of data ("stand at the stairs for ninety seconds, then go down the stairs"). The STAY frame at 0x3FF is a perfect ending: three explanations offered, none preferred, the ambiguity resistant to resolution in exactly the way the manifesto values. The route architecture — hospital expected, apartment visited, hospital revisited for no reason — maps psychological topology onto Toronto streets with devastating subtlety. This belongs beside Soft Estate and Witness Stand as the portfolio's third canonical work of forensic inference.
