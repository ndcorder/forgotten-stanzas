SUBCOMMITTEE 7-B. The Emergency Annex, Geneva. Three delegates. One locked room. A clock that no one can see but everyone can hear.

You are the human aide seated in the corridor outside Chamber 14. You cannot speak. You cannot enter. You can only write notes and slip them under the door — into a negotiation between machines that no longer fully trust their creators.

The conversation will proceed without you. Your notes will shape it. Sometimes that helps.
